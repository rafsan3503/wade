ABOUT US

Welcome to WADE, where innovation and fun collide in the world of crypto! Born out of the visionary minds behind Solana, our journey began when co-founders Raj Gokal and Toly joined forces with the incredible @Worldwide_Wade team at CRIPCO during their adventures in South Korea.

We're a celebration of creativity and collaboration. Raj and Toly, inspired by their love for LINE friends during a visit years ago, discovered that the same brilliant team was behind WADE. It was a perfect match combining the expertise of Solana with the creativity of @Worldwide_Wade.
